The Indian Rupee on the Blockchain

Indicoin brings the stability of the Indian Rupee to the decentralized world, enabling borderless transactions and participation in the decentralized finance ecosystem.

Our Mission

To bridge the gap between traditional finance and the decentralized world by providing a stable, reliable, and accessible digital representation of the Indian Rupee on the blockchain.
